Meet the team

Finding Esteemed talent is our talent.

Our story begins with two software developers who met through Drupal events, and discovered they had quite a bit in common.

Chris McGrath and Albert Volkman founded Esteemed to address their shared interest. Together with a team of ambitious go-getters, they built one of the world’s largest Drupal talent networks

Our values

Results

Work can be challenging. Esteemed works tirelessly to provide tools that increase effectiveness in the workplace and the quality of life of our users.

Passion

We search for team members who have a passion for solving problems for clients, while collaborating with and mentoring fellow colleagues.

Simplicity

As the workplace becomes increasingly complex, we believe access to advice, support, and the right tools makes all the difference.

We've built an Esteemed network for contractors and employers alike

Esteemed exists to solve the very issue AI and Chris noticed while working at a Seattle-based digital agency. Employers in the digital space struggled to hire Esteemed talent.

There simply weren’t enough developers to fill open roles, and anyone good was already busy.

The solution? A platform where advice support, tools, and people can come together.
