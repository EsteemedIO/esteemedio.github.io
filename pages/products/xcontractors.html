[x]Contractors

Find the Right Resources with Esteemed

[x]Contractors was founded by Chris McGrath and Albert Volkman to address a shared concern: there simply weren’t enough developers for employers to fill open roles, and top-performing talent was already booked out.

The solution? A platform that centralizes advice, support, tools, and people.

Why [x]Contractors?

We strive to lower your hiring risk by building a vast network of high-quality, pre-screened digital professionals in hard-to-hire niches, available for whatever work your team can’t handle. We started with Drupal, and made our way into WordPress. Now we’re taking on Python, e-commerce, .NET, and many more technologies — all under the [x]Contractors brand.
