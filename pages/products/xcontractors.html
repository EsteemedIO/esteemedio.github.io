[x]Contractors

Chris McGrath and Albert Volkman founded [x]Contractors to address their shared interests–finding employers in the digital space top-performing talent. There simply weren’t enough developers to fill open roles, and anyone good was already busy.

The solution? A platform where advice, support, tools, and people can come together.

Why [x]Contractors?

We strive to lower your hiring risk by building a vast network of high-quality, pre-screened digital professionals in hard-to-hire niches, available for whatever work your team can’t handle. We started with Drupal, and made our way into WordPress. Now we’re taking on python, e-commerce, .NET, and many more technologies–all under the [x]Contractors brand.
